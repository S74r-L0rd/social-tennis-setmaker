# Team Meeting Schedule

The team holds a weekly meeting every Thursday at **5:00 PM**. 
Meeting minutes will be recorded during each meeting and shared with the team to ensure transparency and accountability. 
In addition, the meeting records will be regularly uploaded to the **meeting-minutes** directory in the project’s GitHub repository to ensure proper documentation and version control.

https://github.com/S74r-L0rd/social-tennis-setmaker/tree/meeting-minutes

| Meeting Type | Schedule | Format | Purpose |
|--------------|----------|--------|---------|
| Weekly Team Meeting | Every Thursday, 5:00 PM | In-person / Online | Review progress, assign tasks, discuss technical issues, and plan upcoming work |
